What is pair programming?

Working with someone else to increase the quality of code output. It allows you to have two sets of eyes on one project, one person can focus on the syntax, and the other can focus on the global picture. At the same time both can be checking the other persons work.

Why is pair programming important to DBC?

It is diffficult for a master to explain what they can easily do. People learning together can more easily sympathize with the other's situation and therefore can often articulate a problem more clearly.

Compare Driver and Navigator

A driver is the one typing and focusing on the syntax. The navigator primarily focuses on the overall picture in mind.
